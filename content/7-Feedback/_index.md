### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at Amazon Web Services (AWS) Vietnam was incredibly professional, dynamic, and open. As a frontend developer intern, I felt welcomed from day one. Everyone in the office was willing to support me whenever I faced complex UI/UX or technical challenges. The modern workspace and access to advanced collaboration tools provided a highly productive atmosphere that helped me stay focused and motivated throughout the internship.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided outstanding technical and professional guidance. Whenever I had questions regarding UI layout adjustments, responsive design optimization, or client-side API integration, my mentor explained the concepts clearly and guided me toward the best practices. I am particularly grateful that my mentor encouraged me to read backend documentation and system logic independently to map data correctly, rather than just giving me the answers. The admin team was also extremely helpful, ensuring smooth onboarding and supporting us with all necessary resources.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned to me were directly relevant to my academic major in Information Technology/Software Engineering. Developing frontend features, optimizing interfaces for multiple screen sizes, and handling API communication allowed me to apply my university knowledge to a real-world project (GreenLens Kids) while expanding my skills in modern web development standards.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
This internship offered immense learning opportunities. On the technical side, I significantly improved my skills in frontend development, responsive design, UI/UX refinement, and error handling. On the soft skills side, I learned how to collaborate effectively within an Agile team, communicate efficiently with backend developers regarding API contracts, and manage my daily tasks to meet project deadlines.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture at AWS Vietnam is exceptionally positive and inclusive, heavily reflecting its core leadership principles. Team spirit was strong; despite being an intern and a regular team member, my opinions during UI/UX and technical discussions were always respected. When deadlines for the Admin Dashboard and core features approached, the entire team collaborated closely and supported each other seamlessly.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The internship policies were highly supportive, offering flexible working arrangements that allowed me to balance my responsibilities. Additionally, having the opportunity to learn from internal technical shares and gain insights into the AWS ecosystem was a major benefit that added incredible value to my learning journey.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
The most satisfying part was seeing the direct impact of my work on the GreenLens Kids project. Witnessing the Admin Dashboard transform into a clean, responsive, and intuitive interface based on team feedback was incredibly rewarding. I am also highly satisfied with the professional growth I experienced by learning how frontend interfaces align perfectly with backend data structures.
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
I think the company could incorporate regular Code Review sessions or technical workshops directly led by Senior Engineers or Project Mentors. While the initial self-paced learning paths and documentations are highly valuable, having experienced engineers directly review our source code, point out 'blind spots', and guide us on production-grade standards would help interns adopt optimization mindsets and clean up the codebase in a much faster and more practical manner.
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Absolutely. I would highly recommend this internship program to anyone passionate about software development. AWS Vietnam provides a world-class working environment, exceptional mentorship, and practical project experiences that truly bridge the gap between academic theory and industry reality.

### Suggestions & Expectations
Would you like to continue this program in the future?
Yes. If given the opportunity, I would love to continue working or collaborating with the team in the future. This experience has been pivotal in helping me grow both as a frontend developer and as a professional in the IT industry.
